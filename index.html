Hi, I’m Nathan Doyle. I write fiction, poetry, and flash that cuts close to the bone. My work lives at the intersection of silence and survival—crafted with grit, clarity, and raw emotional weight.

Whether it’s a poem that stings or a story that smolders, I write to connect. I write to remember. I write because language has teeth, and I’m not afraid to get bit.
